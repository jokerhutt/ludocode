## 6. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time.

Upon deletion:

- Your personal data (email, display name, account identifiers) will be removed from our active systems.  
- Associated user content will be deleted or anonymized.  
- Limited data may be retained where required by law or necessary for security and abuse prevention.  

Backup systems may retain data temporarily but are automatically overwritten over time.
